Book 1 – Kitab-ul-Wahy (Beginning of Revelation)

Is section me Sahih al-Bukhari ki hadith ek-ek karke add ki jayengi.

Hadith 1 – Niyyah (Intention)

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّمَا الْأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ

Tarjuma

Har amal ka daromadar niyyat par hai.

Aasan Samajh

Har nek amal sirf Allah Ta'ala ki raza ke liye hona chahiye. Amal ki qabooliyat niyyat par mabni hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 1

Hadith 2 – Islam, Iman Aur Ihsan

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

هَذَا جِبْرِيلُ أَتَاكُمْ يُعَلِّمُكُمْ دِينَكُمْ

Tarjuma

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Ye Jibreel the, jo tumhare paas tumhe tumhara Deen sikhane aaye the."

Aasan Samajh

Is mashhoor hadith me Hazrat Jibreel عليه السلام ne Islam, Iman aur Ihsan ke baare me sawal kiye. Is hadith se hame Deen ki bunyadi taleem milti hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 50
📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 9

Hadith 3 – Ilm Hasil Karne Ki Fazilat

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

مَنْ يُرِدِ اللَّهُ بِهِ خَيْرًا يُفَقِّهْهُ فِي الدِّينِ

Tarjuma

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jis ke saath Allah Ta'ala bhalai ka irada karta hai, use Deen ki samajh ata farmata hai."

Aasan Samajh

Deen ka ilm hasil karna bahut badi ne'mat hai. Jo shakhs Qur'an aur Sunnat ko samajhne ki koshish karta hai, Allah Ta'ala uske liye bhalai ka irada farmata hai.

Faida

✅ Deen ka ilm hasil karo.
✅ Qur'an aur Hadith ko samajh kar amal karo.
✅ Ilm dusron tak bhi pahunchao.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 71
📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 1037

Hadith 4 – Behtareen Musalman Kaun?

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

الْمُسْلِمُ مَنْ سَلِمَ الْمُسْلِمُونَ مِنْ لِسَانِهِ وَيَدِهِ

Tarjuma

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Woh Musalman hai jiske haath aur zubaan se doosre Musalman mehfooz rahen."

Aasan Samajh

Ek sachcha Musalman kisi ko apni baat ya apne amal se takleef nahi deta. Achhi zubaan aur achha akhlaq Islam ki badi taleem hai.

Faida

✅ Kisi ko gaali ya takleef na do.
✅ Apni zubaan ki hifazat karo.
✅ Achhe akhlaq se pesh aao.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 10
📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 40

Hadith 5 – Islam Ki Bunyad Paanch Cheezon Par Hai

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

بُنِيَ الْإِسْلَامُ عَلَى خَمْسٍ

Tarjuma

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Islam ki bunyad paanch cheezon par hai: Allah ke siwa kisi ko mabood na maanna aur Muhammad ﷺ ko Allah ka Rasool maanna, Namaz qayam karna, Zakat dena, Hajj karna aur Ramazan ke roze rakhna."

Aasan Samajh

Ye Islam ke paanch bunyadi arkaan hain. Har Musalman par in par amal karna zaroori hai (jahan farz ki shartein poori hoti hon).

Faida

✅ Kalima par mazboot iman rakho.
✅ Panch waqt ki namaz padho.
✅ Zakat ada karo (agar farz ho).
✅ Ramazan ke roze rakho.
✅ Istita'at ho to Hajj karo.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 8
📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 16

Hadith 6 – Deen Aasaan Hai

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّ الدِّينَ يُسْرٌ

Tarjuma

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Beshak Deen aasan hai. Jo shakhs Deen me sakhti ikhtiyar karega, Deen us par ghalib aa jayega. Isliye durust tareeqe par chalo, qareeb raho aur khushkhabri do."

Aasan Samajh

Islam fitrat ka Deen hai. Allah Ta'ala ne Deen ko insaan ke liye aasan banaya hai. Ibadat me ghuluw (beja sakhti) se bachna chahiye aur Sunnat ke mutabiq amal karna chahiye.

Faida

✅ Deen par aasan tareeqe se amal karo.
✅ Sunnat ke mutabiq zindagi guzaro.
✅ Apne aur dusron ke liye Deen ko mushkil na banao.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 39
