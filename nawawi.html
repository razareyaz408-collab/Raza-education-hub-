🌙 40 Hadith Nawawi

Authentic Hadith with Easy Explanation

Hadith 1 – Niyyat (Intention)

Har amal ka daromadar niyyat par hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّمَا الْأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ، وَإِنَّمَا لِكُلِّ امْرِئٍ مَا نَوَى

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Amal ka daromadar niyyat par hai, aur har shakhs ko wahi milega jis ki usne niyyat ki."

Aasan Samajh

Allah Ta'ala amal se pehle niyyat ko dekhta hai. Agar koi nek kaam sirf Allah ki raza ke liye kiya jaye to uska ajr milta hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 1
📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 1907

Hadith 3 – Islam ke Paanch Arkaan

Islam ki bunyad paanch cheezon par hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

بُنِيَ الإِسْلَامُ عَلَى خَمْسٍ

Tarjuma

Islam ki bunyad paanch cheezon par rakhi gayi hai.

Aasan Samajh

Islam ke paanch bunyadi arkaan hain: Shahadat, Namaz, Zakat, Roza aur Hajj (jo qudrat rakhta ho).

Hawala:
📚 Sahih al-Bukh

Hadith 2 – Deen Khair-Khwahi Hai

Deen ka bunyadi hissa sachchi khair-khwahi hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

الدِّينُ النَّصِيحَةُ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Deen khair-khwahi (sachchi naseehat aur bhalai chahna) ka naam hai."

Aasan Samajh

Ek Musalman Allah, Uske Rasool ﷺ, Qur'an, Musalman hukmaranon aur tamam Musalmanon ke liye ikhlaas aur bhalai ka jazba rakhe.

Hawala

📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 55

Hadith 3 – Islam ke Paanch Arkaan

Islam ki buniyad paanch arkaan par hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

بُنِيَ الْإِسْلَامُ عَلَى خَمْسٍ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Islam ki buniyad paanch cheezon par rakhi gayi hai."

Aasan Samajh

Islam ke paanch buniyadi arkaan hain:
• Kalima (Shahadat)
• Namaz
• Zakat
• Ramazan ke Roze
• Hajj (jo qudrat rakhta ho)

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 8
📚 Sahih Muslim: 16
