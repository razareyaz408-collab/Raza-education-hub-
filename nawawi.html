🌙 40 Hadith Nawawi

Authentic Hadith with Easy Explanation

Hadith 1 - Niyyah (Intention)

Har amal ka daromadar niyyat par hai.

Hawala: Sahih al-Bukhari: 1, Sahih Muslim: 1907

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّمَا الْأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ

Tarjuma

Har amal ka daromadar niyyat par hai.

Aasan Samajh

Agar koi amal Allah ki raza ke liye kiya jaye to uska ajr milega.

Hawala: Sahih al-Bukhari: 1, Sahih Muslim: 1907

Hadith 2

Islam ke bunyadi usoolon par mabni hadith.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

الدِّينُ النَّصِيحَةُ

Tarjuma

Deen khair-khwahi ka naam hai.

Hawala: Sahih Muslim: 55

Hadith 3
