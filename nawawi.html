🌙 40 Hadith Nawawi

Authentic Hadith with Easy Explanation

Hadith 1 – Niyyat (Intention)

Har amal ka daromadar niyyat par hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّمَا الْأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ، وَإِنَّمَا لِكُلِّ امْرِئٍ مَا نَوَى

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Amal ka daromadar niyyat par hai, aur har shakhs ko wahi milega jis ki usne niyyat ki."

Aasan Samajh

Allah Ta'ala amal se pehle niyyat ko dekhta hai. Agar koi nek kaam sirf Allah ki raza ke liye kiya jaye to uska ajr milta hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 1
📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 1907

Hadith 3 – Islam ke Paanch Arkaan

بُنِيَ الإِسْلَامُ عَلَى خَمْسٍ

Aasan Samajh

Islam ke paanch bunyadi arkaan hain: Shahadat, Namaz, Zakat, Roza aur Hajj (jo qudrat rakhta ho).

Hawala:
📚 Sahih al-Bukh

Hadith 2 – Deen Khair-Khwahi Hai

Hadith 4 – Insaan ki Takhleeq

Insaan ki paidaish aur taqdeer ka bayan.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّ أَحَدَكُمْ يُجْمَعُ خَلْقُهُ فِي بَطْنِ أُمِّهِ أَرْبَعِينَ يَوْمًا

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Tum me se har ek ki paidaish maa ke pet me chalis din tak jama ki jati hai. Phir Allah Ta'ala farishte ko bhejta hai jo uski taqdeer likhta hai."

Aasan Samajh

Allah Ta'ala insaan ko paida hone se pehle hi uski umr, rizq, amal aur anjaam ka ilm rakhta hai. Isliye hamesha nek amal karne ki koshish karni chahiye.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 3208
📚 Sahih Muslim: 2643

Hadith 5 – Bid'at se Bachna

Deen me nayi baat shamil karna qabool nahi.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

مَنْ أَحْدَثَ فِي أَمْرِنَا هَذَا مَا لَيْسَ مِنْهُ فَهُوَ رَدٌّ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Jis ne hamare is deen me koi aisi nayi baat nikali jo isme se nahi hai, to woh mardood (qabool nahi) hai."

Aasan Samajh

Islam me ibadat aur deen ke mamlaat me wahi tareeqa apnana chahiye jo Qur'an aur Sunnat se sabit ho. Deen me apni taraf se nayi baat shamil nahi karni chahiye.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 2697
📚 Sahih Muslim: 1718

Hadith 6 – Halal aur Haram Wazeh Hain

Shak wali cheezon se bachne ki taleem.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّ الْحَلَالَ بَيِّنٌ وَإِنَّ الْحَرَامَ بَيِّنٌ، وَبَيْنَهُمَا أُمُورٌ مُشْتَبِهَاتٌ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Halal wazeh hai aur Haram bhi wazeh hai. In dono ke darmiyan kuch shak wali cheezen hain jinhe bahut se log nahi jaante."

Aasan Samajh

Agar kisi cheez ke halal ya haram hone me shak ho, to usse bachna behtar hai. Isse insaan apne deen aur apni izzat ki hifazat karta hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 52
📚 Sahih Muslim: 1599

Hadith 7 – Deen Khair-Khwahi Hai

Har Musalman ko Allah aur uske bandon ke liye khair-khwahi rakhni chahiye.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

الدِّينُ النَّصِيحَةُ، قُلْنَا: لِمَنْ؟ قَالَ: لِلَّهِ، وَلِكِتَابِهِ، وَلِرَسُولِهِ، وَلِأَئِمَّةِ الْمُسْلِمِينَ وَعَامَّتِهِمْ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Deen khair-khwahi ka naam hai." Sahaba ne pucha: "Kis ke liye?" Aap ﷺ ne farmaya: "Allah ke liye, Uski Kitab ke liye, Uske Rasool ﷺ ke liye, Musalman hukmaranon ke liye aur aam Musalmanon ke liye."

Aasan Samajh

Ek sachcha Musalman hamesha Allah ki ita'at karta hai, Qur'an par amal karta hai, Rasoolullah ﷺ ki Sunnat par chalta hai aur sab Musalmanon ki bhalai chahta hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 55

Hadith 8 – Islam ke Bunyadi Huqooq

Kalima, Namaz, Zakat aur Musalman ki jaan-o-maal ki hifazat.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

أُمِرْتُ أَنْ أُقَاتِلَ النَّاسَ حَتَّى يَشْهَدُوا أَنْ لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَأَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا رَسُولُ اللَّهِ، وَيُقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ، وَيُؤْتُوا الزَّكَاةَ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Mujhe hukm diya gaya hai ke logon ko Allah ki wahdaniyat aur Muhammad ﷺ ki Risalat ki gawahi dene, Namaz qayam karne aur Zakat ada karne tak dawat doon. Jab woh ye kar lenge to unki jaan aur maal mehfooz ho jayenge, siwaye shar'i haq ke."

Aasan Samajh

Islam me Kalima, Namaz aur Zakat bahut aham hain. Jo Musalman in bunyadi farz ko qabool karta hai, uski jaan aur maal ki hifazat ki jati hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 25
📚 Sahih Muslim: 22

Hadith 9 – Jis Cheez Se Mana Kiya Gaya Ho Usse Bacho

Allah aur Rasool ﷺ ke ahkam par amal karna.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

مَا نَهَيْتُكُمْ عَنْهُ فَاجْتَنِبُوهُ، وَمَا أَمَرْتُكُمْ بِهِ فَأْتُوا مِنْهُ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُمْ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jis cheez se main tumhe mana karun usse bach jao, aur jis cheez ka hukm doon usme apni taqat ke mutabiq amal karo."

Aasan Samajh

Musalman ko haram aur mana ki hui cheezon se bachna chahiye. Aur Allah aur Uske Rasool ﷺ ke hukm par apni poori koshish ke saath amal karna chahiye.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 7288
📚 Sahih Muslim: 1337

Hadith 10 – Pak Rizq aur Dua ki Qubooliyat

Halal kamai aur paak ghiza ki ahmiyat.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّ اللَّهَ طَيِّبٌ لَا يَقْبَلُ إِلَّا طَيِّبًا

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Beshak Allah Ta'ala Paak hai aur sirf paak (halal) cheez hi qubool farmata hai."

Aasan Samajh

Musalman ko hamesha halal kamai karni chahiye aur halal khana khana chahiye. Haram kamai aur haram ghiza insaan ki duaon ki qubooliyat me rukawat ban sakti hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 1015

Hadith 11 – Shak Wali Cheezon Ko Chhod Do

Jo cheez shak me daale, use chhod dena behtar hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

دَعْ مَا يَرِيبُكَ إِلَى مَا لَا يَرِيبُكَ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Jo cheez tumhe shak me daale use chhod do, aur us cheez ko apnao jisme shak na ho."

Aasan Samajh

Agar kisi kaam ya cheez ke sahi ya galat hone me dil me shak paida ho, to usse bachna behtar hai. Isse insaan apne deen aur akhlaq ki hifazat karta hai.

Hawala

📚 Jami' at-Tirmidhi: 2518
📚 Sunan an-Nasa'i: 5711

Hadith 12 – Be-Fayda Baaton Ko Chhodna

Ek achhe Musalman ki nishani hai ke woh be-fayda baaton aur kaamon ko chhod de.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

مِنْ حُسْنِ إِسْلَامِ الْمَرْءِ تَرْكُهُ مَا لَا يَعْنِيهِ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Insaan ke Islam ki khoobsurti ye hai ke woh un baaton aur kaamon ko chhod de jo uske kisi kaam ke nahi hain."

Aasan Samajh

Musalman ko apna waqt sirf achhe aur faydemand kaamon me lagana chahiye. Be-fayda baatein, jhagde aur fazool kaam se bachna ek achhe Musalman ki pehchan hai.

Hawala

📚 Jami' at-Tirmidhi: 2317
📚 Sunan Ibn Majah: 3976

Hadith 13 – Apne Bhai Ke Liye Wahi Pasand Karo

Iman ki kamal ki nishani.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

لَا يُؤْمِنُ أَحَدُكُمْ حَتَّى يُحِبَّ لِأَخِيهِ مَا يُحِبُّ لِنَفْسِهِ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Tum me se koi shakhs us waqt tak kamil iman wala nahi ho sakta jab tak woh apne bhai ke liye wahi pasand na kare jo apne liye pasand karta hai."

Aasan Samajh

Ek Musalman ko hasad aur bughz se bachna chahiye. Dusre Musalman ke liye bhi wahi bhalai chahe jo apne liye chahta hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 13
📚 Sahih Muslim: 45

Hadith 14 – Musalman ke Khoon ki Hurmat

Islam me jaan ki hifazat ki ahmiyat.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

لَا يَحِلُّ دَمُ امْرِئٍ مُسْلِمٍ يَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَأَنِّي رَسُولُ اللَّهِ إِلَّا بِإِحْدَى ثَلَاثٍ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Jo Musalman Allah ki wahdaniyat aur meri Risalat ki gawahi deta ho, uska khoon (jaan lena) halal nahi, siwaye teen shar'i surat ke."

Aasan Samajh

Islam insani jaan ki hifazat ka hukm deta hai. Kisi Musalman ki jaan lena bahut bada jurm hai. Sirf shar'i adalat aur shar'i qanoon ke mutabiq hi un khaas suraton me faisla hota hai jinhe Hadith me bayan kiya gaya hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 6878
📚 Sahih Muslim: 1676

Hadith 15 – Achhi Baat Kaho Ya Khamosh Raho

Achhi guftagu aur mehmaan-nawazi ki taleem.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

مَنْ كَانَ يُؤْمِنُ بِاللَّهِ وَالْيَوْمِ الْآخِرِ فَلْيَقُلْ خَيْرًا أَوْ لِيَصْمُتْ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

"Jo Allah aur Aakhirat ke din par iman rakhta ho, use chahiye ke achhi baat kahe ya khamosh rahe."

Aasan Samajh

Musalman ko apni zubaan ki hifazat karni chahiye. Ghibat, jhoot, gaali aur buri baaton se bachna chahiye. Agar achhi baat na ho to khamosh rehna behtar hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: 6018
📚 Sahih Muslim: 47

Hadith 16 – Gussa Mat Karo

Gusse par qabu rakhna momin ki achhi sifat hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

لَا تَغْضَبْ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Gussa mat karo."

Aasan Samajh

Ek Sahabi ne Rasulullah ﷺ se naseehat maangi. Aap ﷺ ne kai baar farmaya: "Gussa mat karo." Yani Musalman ko apne gusse par qabu rakhna chahiye aur sabr se kaam lena chahiye.

Faida

✅ Gusse par qabu rakhna Allah Ta'ala ko pasand hai.
✅ Sabr aur maafi momin ki pehchan hai.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 6116

Hadith 17 – Har Kaam Ihsan Ke Saath Karo

Har kaam behtareen tareeqe se karne ki taleem.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

إِنَّ اللَّهَ كَتَبَ الْإِحْسَانَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Beshak Allah Ta'ala ne har kaam me ihsan (behtareen tareeqa) ko farz kiya hai."

Aasan Samajh

Musalman ko har kaam imandari, rahm aur behtareen tareeqe se karna chahiye. Kisi ke saath zulm ya sakhti nahi karni chahiye.

Faida

✅ Har kaam me imandari aur behtareen akhlaq apnao.
✅ Janwaron ke saath bhi rahm aur narmi se pesh aao.

Hawala

📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 1955

Hadith 18 – Allah Se Daro Aur Nek Akhlaq Apnao

Taqwa aur achhe akhlaq ki taleem.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

اتَّقِ اللَّهَ حَيْثُمَا كُنْ، وَأَتْبِعِ السَّيِّئَةَ الْحَسَنَةَ تَمْحُهَا، وَخَالِقِ النَّاسَ بِخُلُقٍ حَسَنٍ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jahan bhi ho Allah se darte raho. Bure amal ke baad nek amal karo, woh us burai ko mita dega. Aur logon ke saath achhe akhlaq se pesh aao."

Aasan Samajh

Har haal me Allah ka khauf aur uski ita'at ikhtiyar karo. Agar koi gunah ho jaye to foran taubah aur nek amal karo. Har insaan ke saath narmi, izzat aur achhe akhlaq se pesh aao.

Faida

✅ Hamesha Allah se daro.
✅ Gunah ke baad taubah aur nek amal karo.
✅ Sabke saath husn-e-akhlaq se pesh aao.

Hawala

📚 Jami' at-Tirmidhi: Hadith 1987

Hadith 19 – Allah Ki Hifazat Karo

Allah ke ahkaam ki hifazat karne wale ki Allah hifazat farmata hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

احْفَظِ اللَّهَ يَحْفَظْكَ، احْفَظِ اللَّهَ تَجِدْهُ تُجَاهَكَ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne Hazrat Abdullah ibn Abbas رضي الله عنه se farmaya: "Allah (ke ahkaam) ki hifazat karo, Allah tumhari hifazat karega. Allah (ke huqooq) ki hifazat karo, tum use apne saath paoge."

Aasan Samajh

Jo shakhs Allah Ta'ala ke hukmon par amal karta hai, namaz, roza aur dusre farz ada karta hai aur gunahon se bachta hai, Allah Ta'ala uski duniya aur aakhirat me madad aur hifazat farmata hai.

Faida

✅ Allah par bharosa rakho.
✅ Har haal me Allah ke ahkaam par amal karo.
✅ Mushkil waqt me sirf Allah se madad maango.

Hawala

📚 Jami' at-Tirmidhi: Hadith 2516

Hadith 20 – Haya Iman Ka Hissa Hai

Haya (sharm-o-haya) Musalman ki khoobsurat sifat hai.

إِذَا لَمْ تَسْتَحْيِ فَاصْنَعْ مَا شِئْتَ

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jab tum me haya na rahe, to jo chaho karo."

Aasan Samajh

Haya ek momin ki bahut badi ne'mat hai. Jo shakhs Allah Ta'ala se haya karta hai, woh gunahon se bachta hai aur hamesha nek kaam karta hai.

Faida

✅ Haya iman ki khoobsurat sifat hai.
✅ Gunahon se bachne me haya madad karti hai.
✅ Har kaam Allah ki raza ke mutabiq karna chahiye.

Hawala

📚 Sahih al-Bukhari: Hadith 3483

Faida

Hadith 22 – Jannat Tak Pahunchane Wale A'maal

Farz ibadat ki pabandi Jannat ka rasta hai.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

أَرَأَيْتَ إِذَا صَلَّيْتُ الْمَكْتُوبَاتِ، وَصُمْتُ رَمَضَانَ، وَأَحْلَلْتُ الْحَلَالَ، وَحَرَّمْتُ الْحَرَامَ، وَلَمْ أَزِدْ عَلَى ذَلِكَ شَيْئًا، أَأَدْخُلُ الْجَنَّةَ؟ قَالَ: نَعَمْ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Ek Sahabi ne pucha: "Agar main farz namazen padhun, Ramazan ke roze rakhun, halal ko halal aur haram ko haram maanun aur isse zyada na karun, to kya main Jannat me dakhil ho jaunga?" Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Haan."

Aasan Samajh

Jo Musalman imandari ke saath farz ibadat ada kare, halal aur haram ka khayal rakhe aur Allah Ta'ala ke ahkam par chale, uske liye Jannat ki badi umeed hai.

Faida

✅ Farz Namaz ki pabandi karo.
✅ Ramazan ke roze rakho.
✅ Halal aur Haram ka khayal rakho.
✅ Allah Ta'ala ke ahkam par amal karo.

Hawala

📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 15

Hadith 23 – Pakizgi Iman Ka Hissa Hai

Pakizgi, Zikr aur Namaz ki fazilat.

📖 Hadith Padhein

Arabic

الطُّهُورُ شَطْرُ الْإِيمَانِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ تَمْلَأُ الْمِيزَانَ، وَسُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ تَمْلَآنِ مَا بَيْنَ السَّمَاءِ وَالْأَرْضِ، وَالصَّلَاةُ نُورٌ، وَالصَّدَقَةُ بُرْهَانٌ، وَالصَّبْرُ ضِيَاءٌ، وَالْقُرْآنُ حُجَّةٌ لَكَ أَوْ عَلَيْكَ

Tarjuma (Aasan Hindi)

Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Pakizgi aadha iman hai. 'Alhamdulillah' mizan ko bhar deta hai. 'Subhanallah' aur 'Alhamdulillah' aasman aur zameen ke darmiyan ko bhar dete hain. Namaz roshni hai, Sadaqah daleel hai, Sabr roshni hai aur Qur'an tumhare haq me ya tumhare khilaf hujjat hoga."

Aasan Samajh

Islam safai aur pakizgi ki taleem deta hai. Namaz, Sadaqah, Sabr aur Qur'an par amal karna ek momin ki kamyabi ka zariya hai.

Faida

✅ Pakizgi ka khayal rakho.
✅ Namaz ki pabandi karo.
✅ Sabr aur Sadaqah ki adat banao.
✅ Roz Qur'an padho aur us par amal karo.

Hawala

📚 Sahih Muslim: Hadith 223

Hadith 24 – Zulm Haram Hai (Hadith Qudsi)
